Our Services

We render the following services:

  • Holiday Classes

    This programme is open to children of age 5 and above.
    Children are taught to play any of the following instruments;
    Saxophone, Drums, Keyboard, Violin and more within the holiday period.
    This programme is also available to adults regardless of age.

  • Children School-hour Classes

    This programme is open to schools with vacancies
    We teach children in different schools during school
    hours to play musicl instruments of their choice

  • Home Classes

    Interested parents can hire us to teach their children
    the musical instrument of their choice.

  • Church Training

    We train interested members of different churches
    to play the musical instrument of their choice

  • Special Events

    We play and supply skilled and talented musical instrumentalists
    to people who may need their services for special events
    like birthday parties, weddings, burials etc.
